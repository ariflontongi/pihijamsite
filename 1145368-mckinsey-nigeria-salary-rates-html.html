Let's talk about what you can earn at McKinsey!

    McKinsey - one of the best companies in Nigeria

    McKinsey is one of the fastest growing companies in the world. In 2002, McKinsey entered Nigerian market. McKinsey is considered the global champion regarding multinational companies that have a high impact on Africa market.

Therefore, it's a rare case in Nigeria that McKinsey employees disclose this information. Nonetheless, the information from the South African office can give you the approximate figures!

    Top 5 Positions and Salaries at McKinsey

    Business Analyst

    It's one of the preferable jobs at this company. It's also one of the highest paid jobs in Nigeria. If you look at the Glassdoor website for the approximate figures for this position, you will get 431,200 ZAR which equals N12,502,937.

    Senior Research Analyst

    According to the McKinsey Salary Glassdoor data from South Africa, the approximate salary range starts from 507,000 ZAR per year. Which means that Nigerian Senior Research Analysts may earn about N14,700,800

    3. Engagement Manager

    This manager is responsible for financial aspects of the deal. It includes billing services, follow-up on partners and so on. The estimated salary for this employee is about 864,000 ZAR or N25,168,250 per year.

    4. Junior Business Associate

    If you want to start your career at McKinsey, then one of the best options is the position of Junior Business Analyst. You will need to work as a protégé for experienced business analysts. The average salary of Junior Business Analyst is around 15,000 ZAR or N434,000 per month according to the Glassdoor.

    5. Junior Associate

    The approximate salary for this position is around N13,917,926 per year.
