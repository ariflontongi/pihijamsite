    After days of speculation and rumors, Logic has finally broken silence on the status of his relationship with wife Jessica Andrea. The rapper has taken to Instagram to issue a public statement regarding their break-up rumors, confirming that the two have mutually decided that they are better off as friends. In a lengthy text post, Logic explains the situation to his fans to avoid any gossip or speculation as to the reasons for their split. The Bobby Tarantino II artist writes that the reason for their divorce is simple: "It just didn't work out."

    He continued his statement, "There is no anger involved. No fighting, no cheating, no nothing. We love each other and will continue to support each other for the rest of our lives." Logic reminds his fans not to be sad about the break-up as he and Jessica remain friends throughout the divorce process. Bobby released his most recent mixtape earlier this month, mentioning Jessica in many of the tracks.

    Logic and Jessica Andrea were married in October of 2015 and the two were a huge part of each other's public lives. The rapper usually brought her along for award shows and shared loving photos of the two together for his fans to like on Instagram.
