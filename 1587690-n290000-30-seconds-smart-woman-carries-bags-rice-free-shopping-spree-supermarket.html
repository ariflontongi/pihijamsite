    &quot;N290,000 in 30 Seconds&quot;: Smart Woman Carries Bags of Rice During Free Shopping Spree in Supermarket

    A lucky woman who hawks 'Kunu' was picked to participate in a 30-second free shopping spree at a big supermarket. The woman, Ruth Zephaniah was told that everything she was able to pick and throw into the shopping cart within the stipulated time frame would be paid for.

    21 Aug 2024, 00:00
    • A woman was happy when she was told that she had been selected to participate in a free shopping spree at a supermarket
    • She was told that she was going to spend only 30 seconds for the shopping and anything she was able to pick would be paid for
    • When she entered, the woman concentrated on food items such as rice, vegetable oil, noodles and everything amounted to over N290,000

    A lucky woman who hawks 'Kunu' was picked to participate in a 30-second free shopping spree at a big supermarket.

    The woman, Ruth Zephaniah was told that everything she was able to pick and throw into the shopping cart within the stipulated time frame would be paid for.

    Ruth entered the supermarket excitedly, and when the shopping started, she paid attention to food items.
