    Natti Natasha celebrates her new hit 'No pare' topless on Miami beaches

    Recently, an alleged conversation involving reggaeton artist Natti Natasha surfaced, containing what was purported to be an intimate image of the singer. This conversation also included an audio clip supposedly featuring Natti's voice.

    05 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Recently, an alleged conversation involving reggaeton artist Natti Natasha surfaced, containing what was purported to be an intimate image of the singer.

    This conversation also included an audio clip supposedly featuring Natti's voice.

    "Hi daddy, as always thinking of you. I hope you like it very, very much," the voice said.

    This led internet users to believe that the singer had sent the content to a paramour, potentially betraying her current partner Raphy Pina.

    Natasha herself has come forward to counter these allegations, revealing that it was, in fact, part of a promotional campaign for her new single "No Pare."

    The supposedly leaked image is none other than the cover art for the song, which is now available on all major digital platforms.

    Subsequently, in the aftermath of this incident, the artist celebrated her new song's success on Miami's beaches, playfully shedding her bra to enjoy the company of a friend in a bikini.

    Over the past weeks, the Dominican artist has faced an abundance of comments and conjecture about her personal life.

    Nevertheless, even in the midst of this sensitive period, she has harnessed these events to craft the homemade music video for her latest track "No Pare."
