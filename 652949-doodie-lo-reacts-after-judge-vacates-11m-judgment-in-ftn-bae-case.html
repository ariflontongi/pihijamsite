    The legal saga between Doodie Lo and FTN Bae came to a close when the judge awarded him $11M. However, it appears like he'll have to return to court to defend himself against the allegations of sexual abuse. The OTF maintained his innocence since FTN Bae emerged with the heinous allegations. Once the judge ruled in his favor, he immediately celebrated his victory on Instagram afterward. However, it seems as though the judge had a change of heart since then. According to All Hip Hip, the $11M judgment has now been vacated and FTN Bae will receive a new trial.

    The judge awarded Doodie Lo by default after FTN Bae after she didn’t show up to court. Still, it seemed to be a failure to appear. What’s worse is that she was immediately arrested when she tried to enter the courthouse on the day of the trial. FTN Bae said that she felt Doodie Lo and his attorney attempted to set her up, alleging that they tried to prevent her from testifying during a civil trial in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. Additionally, she suggested that it was part of a bigger plot to silence her after she accused Doodie Lo of sexual abuse. She pointed out that one of the arresting officers was supposed to testify against her.

    Doodie Lo Responds To FTN Bae

    In a series of Instagram posts, Doodie Lo continued to push back against her allegations. He shared a video of FTN Bae filing the police report and claimed she accused her producer of similar crimes. “I never in my life seen a person thsi crazy only thought it was in movies my life is not the only one she tried to ruin blamed my producer which was her son football coach and she blamed prolly 4 more people lol you wanna play ima show and this ain’t bout NO money. This my name,” he wrote in the first post.

    Afterward, Doodie Lo shared four screenshots of alleged police reports filed by FTN Bae accusing others of sexual assault. One of these claims were against her father-in-law. “Can Y’all Ask Her Why She’s Not Speaking On These Other People Who She Accused Of Abusing Her Son Is It Because My Name Has “Clout” That She Could Use For Her Own Wack Rap Career You Got A lot Of Explaining To Do,” he wrote. “Explain Why You Only [cap emoji] in On My Name When You Said All These Other People Did Something You Giving Obsessed And Fatal Attraction Vibes Let’s Talk About It [100 emoji] S/O Public Records Yall Can Slide Through These Are Some People She Lied On This Not Even Everything.” Check his posts out above.
