    Known for his versatility as a singer and producer, he has carved a distinct niche and remains a prominent figure in the music scene.

    Early Life & Education

    Austin Agustin Santos, known by his stage name Arcangel, was born on December 23, 1985.

    He was raised by his mother, Carmen Rosa, and his father, Agustin Santos. Unfortunately, Arcangel also experienced the loss of his brother, whose name and identity have not been confirmed.

    Arcangel attended public schools in New York City and received a high school diploma. Although he did not pursue higher education, his love for music became his main focus, leading him to embark on a successful musical journey.

    Personal Life

    Arcangel is happily married to Alejandra Guzmán. The couple has two beautiful children, Angelica Lucero Santos Figueroa, and Austin Alejandro Santos Pascual.

    Their love and support have played a vital role in Arcangel’s personal and professional life.

    Career

    Arcangel’s career began to take off when he formed a duo with Da la Ghetto, and together, they gained recognition with their song “Pegate,” featured on the popular mixtape album “Sangre Nueva” hosted by Hector El Father.

    This breakthrough laid the foundation for Arcangel’s success as a rapper and singer.

    His style quickly caught the attention of industry professionals, leading to collaborations with renowned artists and the release of numerous hit songs.

    Some of his notable tracks include “Si se Da,” “Pa Que la Pases Bien,” “Me Prefieres a Mi,” “Me acostumbre,” “Sigues Con El,” and “Privado.”

    Arcangel’s music includes various genres, including reggaeton, trap, and hip-hop, resulting in a dynamic and distinct sound that resonates with listeners worldwide.
