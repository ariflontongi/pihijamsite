    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Chris Janson was born in Perryville, Missouri, and developed a passion for music at a young age. He moved to Nashville after high school to pursue his music career and quickly made a name for himself in the local music scene.

    Janson’s music career took off when he signed with BNA Records and released his debut single, “Til a Woman Comes Along,” in 2010.
