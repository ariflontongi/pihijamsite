      Although there's no doubt that Kaia is the spitting image of her mother, the young model caused a stir on social media with fans noting how she channelled Miranda almost flawlessly.

      Kaia and her mum took inspiration from Miranda

      Wearing her brunette locks in tumbling waves sideswept to the side, Kaia's imitation of the Australian beauty's world-famous pout had fans noting she is definitely set to make her own stamp on the world of fashion when she's older.

      It could be that Cindy and her daughter took inspiration from a recent photo on Miranda's own Instagram account, as she posed with a friend, smouldering for the camera and holding a flower similar to the one in Kaia's photo.

      However, some fans have speculated that it could be a tribute to Miranda's campaign for Escada, which saw the former Victoria's Secret angel posing with bouquets of colourful flowers, wearing a variety of chic ensembles in everything from creams to bright shades of pink.

      Kaia has previously paid a similar tribute to her mother

      It's not the first time that Kaia has paid tribute to a supermodel as she continues to share snaps. In a photo shared on her and brother Presley's joint Instagram account, the stylish teenage was pictured in several of Cindy's old clothes, including a particularly striking floral Roberto Cavalli dress.

      The evening dress was first worn by Cindy at the Conde Nast Traveler Readers' Choice Awards in New York City back in 2003, however for the gown's latest outing, Kaia has opted for a paved driveway with a stunning sunset backdrop.
