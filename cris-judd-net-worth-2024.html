    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Cris Judd was born in Abilene, Texas, and spent his early years in various parts of the world due to his father’s military career.     Judd’s talent as a dancer did not go unnoticed. He was soon choreographing for various artists and music videos, further establishing his name in the industry. Their whirlwind romance led to a short-lived marriage, thrusting Judd further into the limelight.
