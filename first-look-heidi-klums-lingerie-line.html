    15 Aug 2024, 00:00

    After a five-year hiatus, Heidi Klum has returned to lingerie modelling to launch her debut underwear range.
     
    The supermodel and TV presenter stars as the face of her own Heidi Klum Intimates collection, designed and created in partnership with leading Australian lingerie brand Bendon.
     
    The campaign, shot by world-renowned photographer Rankin, sees Klum showing off a range of ladylike underwear, inspired by “a romantic journey through France” and the “essence of the Parisian landscape.”
     
    “It was so exciting to shoot the first ever Heidi Klum Intimates campaign with Rankin,” she says of the imagery. “I’ve always loved modelling lingerie and it was fun to get back in front of the camera wearing beautiful pieces.”


     
    Heidi Klum accepted her first modelling contract from Metropolitan Models New York in 1992, and has since become one of the world’s most recognisable faces, particularly through her stint as a Victoria’s Secret Angel and as the host and executive producer of American fashion show Project Runway.
     
    The debut Heidi Klum Intimates collection is due to hit stores this month. Images courtesy of Heidi Klum Intimates.
