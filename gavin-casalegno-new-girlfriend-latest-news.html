    Introduction

    News: Gavin Casalegno, renowned for his role as Jeramiah, has recently made headlines with news of his new girlfriend. As of 2023, it appears that Casalegno is currently single, following his previous long-term relationship with the multi-talented Larsen Thompson, a model, dancer, and actress. While the details of his new girlfriend have not been disclosed, fans eagerly await further updates on Casalegno’s romantic life.

    Early Life and Career Beginnings

    Gavin Casalegno was born on September 2, 1999, and has had a successful career in the entertainment industry. He gained recognition for his role in the Amazon Prime Video series titled “The Summer I Turned Pretty,” a poignant coming-of-age drama. Hailing from Dallas, Texas, Casalegno is the eldest of his two siblings, with his younger brother and sister, Ashlyn Casalegno, also pursuing careers in acting and modeling.

    During his formative years, Casalegno attended Lovejoy High School in Lucas, Texas. It was during this time that he began to explore the world of modeling, thanks to a suggestion from his mother’s friend. At the age of four, Casalegno landed his first modeling gig with JC Penney, marking the start of his journey in the entertainment industry. His early success in the modeling industry led to opportunities in commercials for notable brands like Sony and Papa John’s.

    Rise to Fame

    Seeking greater opportunities, Casalegno later relocated to Los Angeles, where he quickly made his mark in the film industry. By the age of eleven, he had already secured his first film project. Over the years, Casalegno has embraced diverse acting roles, solidifying his reputation as a talented young actor. He portrayed young Shem in the 2014 film “Noah” and young Damon Salvatore in the popular CW television series “The Vampire Diaries” in 2015. His most recent project includes his role as Trevor Strand in the debut season of “Walker” in 2021.

    Success in “The Summer I Turned Pretty”

    In July 2021, Casalegno made a significant career move when he was cast as Jeremiah Fisher, one of the main characters, in an Amazon Prime television series adaptation of Jenny Han’s acclaimed coming-of-age novel, “The Summer I Turned Pretty.” The series premiered on June 17, 2022, and quickly gained popularity, leading to Amazon’s early renewal for a second season. The second season of “The Summer I Turned Pretty” premiered on July 14, 2023, and received impressive viewership ratings, prompting Amazon to renew it for a third season in August 2023.

    Upcoming Projects

    Adding another exciting chapter to his career, Casalegno was recently cast in the biographical film “Queen of the Ring,” centered around WWE Hall of Fame-inducted wrestler Mildred Burke. In this film, Casalegno will portray the younger version of Burke’s son, Joe. This role showcases his versatility as an actor and further diversifies his portfolio.

    Net Worth and Future Endeavors

    With such a successful career, it’s no wonder that Gavin Casalegno has accumulated an estimated net worth of $5 million dollars. This substantial net worth is a testament to his talent and dedication to his craft. As he continues to make waves in the entertainment industry, it will be exciting to see what the future holds for this talented actor.
