    Givenchy Is Reintroducing the Nightingale Bag

    Givenchy may have introduced the Nightingale bag back in Fall 2006, but years later, it's still a hotmust-have for street style stars and A-Listers alike. For its Fall 2016collection, Riccardo Tisci is bringing the It bag back withan updateddesign tweakedto fit its modern-day woman.

    07 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Givenchy may have introduced the Nightingale bag back in Fall 2006, but years later, it's still a hot must-have for street style stars and A-Listers alike. For its Fall 2016 collection, Riccardo Tisci is bringing the It bag back with an updated design tweaked to fit its modern-day woman. 

    "It's normal that people become very attached to their bag, carrying it everyday it becomes almost an extension of them," Tisci said in a statement, "I wanted to do something very special for the fans of the Nightingale," he said of customers who've taken a liking to the bag. 

    The Nightingale, a polished carry-all satchel, is equally as functional as it is versatile, which is proven by its high-profile owners. Take Kendall and Kylie Jenner, for example: one takes the satchel to the airport, while the other carries it with an all-black ensemble for a day out in L.A. It looks like we can thank its A-List fans for inspiring Givenchy to bring the cult-favorite bag back.
