    Gutfeld was born on September 12, 1964 in San Mateo, California, United States, which makes his nationality American. Greg was born to Jacqueline Bernice and Alfred Jack Gutfeld. He attended Junípero Serra High School, after which he went on to the University of California, Berkeley, from which he graduated with a bachelor’s degree in English. While in college, Greg admits to being a conservative.
