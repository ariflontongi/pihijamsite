    Where to find hold itemsin Pokmon Scarlet and Violet

    10 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Hold items are historically an integral part of Pokémon games, and they’re still extremely important in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet. Rather than being found around the world, these powerful items can be bought from Delibird Presents, a specific chain of store found around cities.

    There are three Delibird Presents stops around Paldea: one in Mesagoza, one in Cascafarra, and another in Levincia. Each one sells different items, so you’ll want to make sure to visit all three. You can check their specific locations by zoom in and on the map. If a city has two Delibird Presents locations, don’t worry — they both carry the same items.

    Things like the Destiny Knot are imperative for passing down IVs when breeding, and items like Leftovers are important for competitive battling. Both the King’s Rock and Razor Claw items have effects when they’re given to your Pokémon, but they’re also necessary to evolve Slowpoke and Sneasel, respectively.

    Below we list where you can find each held item buyable from Delibirds Presents. Note that while you can buy them here, we did find some items randomly around Paldea, like Damp Rocks and Soft Sand.
