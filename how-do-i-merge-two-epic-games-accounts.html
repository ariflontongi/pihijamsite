    Unfortunately, there is currently no way to merge two Epic Games accounts. The Player Support team is unable to assist with requests for merging accounts. If you want to connect an Xbox, PlayStation, or Switch account to your Epic Games account, you can do so by going to your Accounts Connections page. However, merging two separate Epic Games accounts is not possible at this time.

    Why did Epic remove account merging?

    The Epic rule book does not allow selling and purchasing of accounts or using an account under a different identity. As soon as word went out about such activities, bans were issued, and as the ultimate step, Account Merging was removed.

    Can you link two Epic Games accounts with the same email?

    No, each Epic Games account can only be associated with a single email address. You cannot have two Fortnite accounts with the same email address. If you try to create a new Fortnite account with an email address that is already in use, you will receive an error notice.

    How do I transfer my Fortnite account to another email?

    To transfer your Fortnite account to another email address, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your Epic Games account and go to the Account portal.
  • In the ACCOUNT INFO section, click the Edit button next to your email address.
  • In the pop-up window, enter your new email address and click CONFIRM.

    • Can I transfer my Fortnite progress to another account?

    Yes, you can transfer your Fortnite progress to another account by linking all your profiles together. By doing this, you can freely jump between platforms without losing any progress. However, please note that each console/platform must be linked to the same Epic Games account.

    Can You Merge Two Fortnite Accounts?

    No, there is currently no way to merge two Fortnite accounts. Account merging has been removed due to the rule against selling and purchasing of accounts or using an account under a different identity.

    How do I unlink an Epic Games account?

    To unlink an Epic Games account, follow these steps:

  • Verify your Email Address.
  • Visit your Connected Accounts Page.
  • Click on the Accounts Tab.
  • Click the Remove button below the platform you want to disconnect.

    • Can you link 2 Epic Games accounts to Xbox?

    No, each Epic Games account can only be linked to one Xbox account. It is not possible to link multiple Epic Games accounts to the same Xbox account.

    What happens if you unlink an epic account?

    If you unlink your console account from your Epic Games account, you may lose access to your game progress and purchases. Your game progress and purchases are stored on your Epic Games account, so it is recommended to keep your accounts linked to maintain access to your data.

    What is account merging?

    Account merging is the process of combining all the information and transactions from one account into another. However, account merging has been removed for Epic Games accounts.

    How much is Fortnite account worth?

    To determine the approximate value of your Fortnite account, you can add up the value of all the cosmetic items you own in V-Bucks. Each 1,000 V-Bucks is approximately worth $7.99. By converting your V-Bucks to dollars, you can estimate the value of your Fortnite account.

    Can I transfer my Epic account from two different consoles?

    Yes, you can transfer your Epic Games account between different consoles if you have linked your Xbox, PlayStation, PC, Switch, or Mobile account to your Epic Games account. This allows you to access your content and progress on all platforms. It is important to note that each console/platform must be linked to the same Epic Games account.

    Can I use the same Epic Games account on two devices?

    Yes, you can use the same Epic Games account on multiple devices. Your profiles and data will be available on all compatible devices.

    Will I lose all my stuff if I unlink my Fortnite account?

    Unlinking your Fortnite account from a platform account will not cause you to lose your stats and cosmetics. Your Fortnite stats and cosmetics are stored on your Epic Games account, so as long as you sign in and play on the Epic Games account that was linked to your platform account, you should still have access to your progress and items.

    What happens if you change your Epic Games email?

    When you change your Epic Games email, you will receive a message with a verification link to the new email address. The change will update your email associated with your Epic Games account.

    How do I merge Xbox accounts?

    You cannot directly merge or combine Xbox accounts. However, if you have multiple accounts, you can share your Xbox subscription and content on your Xbox One or newer console by setting that console as your home Xbox for all your accounts.

    How do I find out what epic account is linked to my Xbox?

    To find out what Epic Games account is linked to your Xbox, you can go to the Accounts & Services option in your Xbox settings. This will show you which accounts and services are connected to your Xbox account.

    Why does it say my Nintendo account has already been associated with another Epic Games account?

    If you see a message stating that your Nintendo account has already been associated with another Epic Games account, it means that your Nintendo account is already linked to a separate Epic Games account or a nameless account. You will need to unlink your Nintendo account from the other account before linking it to a new Epic Games account.

    Can I transfer Vbucks from one account to another?

    No, it is not currently possible to transfer V-Bucks from one account to another. V-Bucks are tied to the specific Epic Games account and cannot be transferred or gifted to another account. You can only use your V-Bucks to make purchases on the account they are associated with.

    Can two people play Fortnite on the same account at once?

    No, two people cannot play Fortnite on the same account at the same time. Each player needs their own separate account to play the game. However, Fortnite does offer split-screen mode on certain platforms, allowing two players to play together on the same console.

    Can you have 2 Epic accounts on one switch?

    No, each Switch user profile can only be associated with one Epic Games account. It is not possible to have multiple Epic Games accounts linked to the same Switch user profile.
