How far should shingles overhang?

30 Aug 2024, 00:00

Answer When installing drip edge flashing, the edge of the shingles should hang over the roof between an inch and an inch and a half — or between a half inch and three-quarters of an inch if no drip edge flashing is used. When there is too much overhang, the shingles may be blown off in heavy winds; when there is too little, water can leak into the rake or fascia boards.
