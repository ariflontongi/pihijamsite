How Does Golf's Four-Ball Better-Ball Format Work?

08 Aug 2024, 00:00

Four-ball is a team format in golf where a team of two golfers each play their own golf ball. The teammate with the lower score on each hole provides that team’s score for that hole.  For this reason, the format is also often called Four-ball Better Ball (4BBB) as the better score of the pair is counted each time. The format can be used in stroke play or match play, such as in the Ryder Cup or Solheim Cup.
