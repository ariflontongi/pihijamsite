    Salon Inspiration: Charlize Theron

    Wavy Bob

    Nearly two years post-chop, Theron's hair has reached bob length, given a slight wave with this side-parted style on The Tonight Show.

    Pixie

    The actress opted for a simple yet sleek pixie, complete with buttery highlights, for this year's Oscars.

    Wavy Updo

    Theron looked radiant in tousled, slicked back curls at Christian Dior's Fall/Winter 2014 haute couture show.

    Short Layers

    Theron shows that short hair doesn't have to lack in texture or shine with this soft, sweeping 'do.

    Curly Quiff

    For the GLAAD Media Awards, Theron kept sides shorn, adding height with curls shaped into a quiff.

    Textured Pixie

    In the early stages of growing out her close crop, Theron's hair was styled into a gamine pixie, given a hint of movement by hair turned upwards at the crown.

    Close Crop

    The star buzzed off her hair with a razor in anticipation of Mad Max: Fury Road, telling InStyle"I had just finished two press tours and my hair was destroyed from heat damage, so I was glad to get rid of it. I was also a new mom and just loved the idea of not having to style my hair every morning."

    Au Naturel

    Theron often opts for a natural look, including her pared down makeup and simple blowout for the 2013 MTV Movie Awards.

    Rolled Updo

    The prim updo and winged shadow helped Theron channel her Evil Queen character from Snow White and the Huntsman at the film's London premiere.

    Layered Lob

    The former model showcased her bombshell looks and layered lob at CinemaCon 2012, where she was presented with the Distinguished Decade of Achievement in Film Award.
