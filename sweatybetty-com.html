    FAQs

    Does Sweaty Betty have free shipping?

    Sweaty Betty does have free shipping on orders of $75 or more. As a luxury sports brand, it’s not difficult to get to this total. If your order is less than $75, then standard shipping costs are $10. Your order should take 4-6 business days to be delivered. 

    Does Sweaty Betty have an email discount?

    You can get 15% off your first order at Sweaty Betty by signing up for their newsletter. All you need to do is go to their website and type your email address into the email box. Sweaty Betty will also keep you updated on exclusive sales and new clothing.  

    Can I get a professional fitness discount at Sweaty Betty?

    Fitness professionals can qualify for a 30% off discount. If you have a qualified fitness teaching certificate, you can access this promotion. You will need to complete an online form and attach a copy of your certificate to confirm your status. 

    Does Sweaty Betty have student discount?

    You can save up to 25% off your online order with Sweaty Betty. Simply log into your Student Beans account to confirm your education status. You will then be able to use your student promo code on your next order. 

    When are the Sweaty Betty sales?

    Sweaty Betty has sales at the end of each season. You will need to type ‘sale’ into their search bar to go to the page. Some of the best times to shop Sweaty Betty for sales are Black Friday and the holidays. We’ve seen discounts before of up to 60% off in seasonal sales. 

    What is the return policy at Sweaty Betty?

    You can return your Sweaty Betty purchase within 45 days. Your item should be in perfect condition, with your proof of purchase. You will need to go to the Sweaty Betty returns portal to print your USPS label.
