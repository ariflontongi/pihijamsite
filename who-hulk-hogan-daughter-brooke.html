    Who is Hulk Hogan's daughter, Brooke?

    BROOKE Hogan, Hulk Hogan's daughter, rose to popularity when she starred alongside her family on the reality show Hogan Knows Best. Here's what else there is to know about the wrestling legend's daughter.

    07 Aug 2024, 00:00

    BROOKE Hogan, Hulk Hogan's daughter, rose to popularity when she starred alongside her family on the reality show Hogan Knows Best.

    Here's what else there is to know about the wrestling legend's daughter.

    Who is Hulk Hogan's daughter, Brooke?

    Brooke Ellen Bollea was born on May 5, 1988, in Tampa, Florida, to parents Hulk Hogan and Linda Claridge. Brooke is an American singer, actress, and television personality.

    While she achieved mainstream popularity after her family starred on Hogan Knows Best, Brooke broke into the entertainment industry with her music.

    She released her debut single, Everything to Me, in July 2004, which made it to the Billboard Hot 100 singles sales chart. She also toured with Hilary Duff and the Backstreet Boys.

    Her debut album, Undiscovered, was released in 2006. Its lead single, About Us, made it to the top 33 of the Billboard charts in August of that year.

    She has one other album, The Redemption, which was released in 2009. But Brooke is consistently making music, having dropped multiple singles since.

    Outside of music, Brooke also starred in her own reality show, Brooke Knows Best, which ran for two seasons from 2008-2009.

    She also once helped promote Total Nonstop Action Wrestling, or TNA, now Impact Wrestling.

    Brooke also has a handful of acting credits under her belt. She appeared in the Netflix series Glow and the film LA Slasher.

    Where is Brooke Hogan now?

    Despite being mostly out of the spotlight, Brooke Hogan has been keeping herself busy.

    She's running an Airbnb design business, managing a clothing line, and making music.

    “I released a song, I’m doing a workout legging line and… Well, I don’t really tell anybody, but I have an interior design business out of Nashville,” she told LA Weekly in August 2020. “So I’ve just been working on growing that, making things bigger and better there too.”

    Her passion, though, lies in writing songs.

    “...I’m trying to find what I really want to focus my energy on. I’m trying to strike a balance where I do the job that makes the money, and I also do the stuff that feeds my soul,” she said.

    She also shared that the drama surrounding her family affected what could have been a successful music career.

    Hulk and Linda ended their marriage in 2007 after the latter filed for divorce. Their split was finalized in 2009.

    “There was just so much convolution with my family, the TV show, my parents' divorce… All the things that were going on behind the scenes during the time that I felt I didn’t stand a chance,” she said.

    “Even if I worked as hard as I possibly could, the forces around me were working against me.”

    Who has Brooke Hogan dated?

    Brooke Hogan was once engaged to former Dallas Cowboys star Phil Costa. They called off their engagement in 2013.
