    NATALIE Pasquarella is a broadcast journalist for NBC New York while her husband, Jamin Pastore, works in the business world.

    The couple began dating in 2007 and tied the knot 2014.

    Who is Jamin Pastore?

    Pastore is the CEO and president of a private equity company, JPCM, according to his LinkedIn.

    The company specializes in "acquiring, operating, and growing a variety of companies with revenues ranging from $5 million to over $150 million," according to the company's LinkedIn page.

    Pastore has been the CEO and president of the company since 2013.

    He also earned a degree in economics, sociology, markets & management from Duke University in 2005.

    How many children do Natalie and Jamin have?

    The couple has two children: Jamin James and Jada Kathryn.

    While Pasquarella was anchoring the 11pm newscast at New York's NBC4, she went into labor with her son shortly after the show ended.

    She waited until the broadcast ended before seeking medical attention.

    According to NBC New York, she was answering a question when she knew she had gone into labor.

    Twelve hours later, she gave birth to Jamin James Pastore, who weighed 5 pounds and 6 ounces.

    Who is Natalie Pasquarella?

    Pasquarella anchors the news report on NBC New York at 4pm, 6pm, and 11pm.

    She also works on the I-Team for the station.

    She has won four New York Emmys due to her breaking news coverage.

    She graduated from Kent State University before working at WSOC-TV and WTOTV-TV.

    She has interviewed a number of high-profile people, including former President Barack Obama.

    She also covered the 2016 presidential election and Pope Francis' historic visit to New York.
